Section Deduction Amount Description

Section 80CCC 150000 Contributions made to certain pension funds
Section 80CCD 150000 Contributions made to the National Pension System (NPS)
Section 80D 25000 Payments made for health insurance premium for self, spouse and dependent children
Section 80DD 75000 Expenses incurred for the maintenance of a disabled dependent
Section 80DDB 40000 Expenses incurred for medical treatment of specified diseases
Section 80E 200000 Interest paid on education loans
Section 80EE 150000 Interest paid on home loans
Section 80GG 0 Rent paid if the individual does not receive any HRA from the employer
Section 80G 0 Donations made to certain charitable institutions and funds
Section 80GGC 2000 Donations made to political parties
Section 80TTA 10000 Interest earned on savings bank account
Section 80TTB 50000 Interest earned on deposit account for senior citizens
